Mission Statement

German American is dedicated to helping individuals, families and businesses achieve greater prosperity and quality of life. Together, our financial team builds lasting client relationships based on integrity, responsive service, innovation, and shared values. We support the communities we serve and believe that when a community thrives, its people prosper.

Business Overview

German American is a very strong financial services provider in southern Indiana and Kentucky, specializing in banking, insurance, investments and wealth management for business and retail customers. In 1910, German American was founded in a community located in the heart of the Ohio Valley Region, which was rich in German American heritage.Today, its headquarters remain in Jasper, Indiana. The employees of German American strive to establish strong relationships by working closely with businesses and individuals and by providing strong financial solutions.

Mission Statement

We educate, empower and inspire our clients to greater levels of assurance by partnering with them to achieve challenging compliance goals.

Business Overview

KirkpatrickPrice is a licensed CPA firm and PCI QSA, providing technology related assurance services to clients worldwide. With over 15 years of experience in information assurance by performing assessments, audits, and tests that strengthen information security and compliance controls, we are on a mission to create a simpler, more accessible way to learn about compliance, start an audit and complete quality testing. KirkpatrickPrice is dedicated to educating and inspiring clients throughout their security and compliance journeys.

Our core services are provided by a team of expert-level Information Security Specialists who typically have 15-20 years of experience in areas such as IT governance, data security, regulatory compliance and cybersecurity. Internally, we also have our own IT department and development team who work to support our team and initiatives as well as develop our client facing tools. As the world of technology rapidly advances our team values continuous learning and certifications in order to identify and protect against the ever-growing threat landscape.

Med Center Health

800 Park St.
Bowling Green, KY 42101

medcenterhealth.org
martcm01@mchealth.net
Mission Statement

The Mission at Med Center Health is to care for people and improve the lives in the communities we serve.

Business Overview

Since 1926, Med Center Health has served as the premier provider of healthcare services in Southcentral Kentucky. With six hospitals, 30 clinics, more than 140 providers, and 27 specialties across a 12-county area, nobody offers a wider range of care, right here.

Our flagship hospital, The Medical Center at Bowling Green, is a hub of forward thinking and innovation. And no matter where they practice, all of our physicians are driven to achieve the highest possible accreditations, equipping them to employ the latest, innovative approaches to healthcare.

Med Center Health’s six hospitals are located in Albany, Bowling Green, Horse Cave, Franklin, and Scottsville. This wide footprint, combined with our commitment to proactively invest in the resources our communities need, helps people heal closer to home. No matter where you live in Southcentral Kentucky, the highest quality care is within your reach.

Our commitment to the communities we serve goes beyond improving the health of our patients. As a not-for-profit institution, the monetary benefits we generate far outweigh the taxes we would pay if we were in business to make money.

Med Center Health also maintains public-private partnerships with the area’s finest academic institutions, such as Western Kentucky University and the University of Kentucky. And we are the only provider in the region that partners with UK HealthCare. This unique relationship increases opportunities for research and technology while gaining access to the latest in evidence-based medicine.

In addition, the University of Kentucky College of Medicine – Bowling Green Campus, the first 4-year regional medical school in Kentucky, is located on the campus of The Medical Center at Bowling Green. As a teaching hospital, The Medical Center is committed to constantly raising the bar to provide better healthcare for residents of Southcentral Kentucky.
